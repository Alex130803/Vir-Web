About the Destination

Gateway to the Largest Lake in the Balkans

Virpazar is the main gateway to Skadar Lake National Park, the largest lake in the Balkans. Surrounded by mountains, historic fortresses, and rich wildlife, Skadar Lake is home to over 280 bird species, traditional fishing villages, and breathtaking landscapes.

Boat tours from Virpazar offer a unique opportunity to explore hidden canals, floating lotus flowers, historic monasteries, and panoramic viewpoints over Albania and Montenegro.
